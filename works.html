Last Event-Bridge It

Round-1 BATTLE OF MINDS,a quiz based on semester and general facts that measured the aptitude skill of participants and shortlisted the teams
Round-1 is categorized into two parallel events:First is the analytical quiz that included 3 members out of 5 team members and rest of the 2 members participated in the technical visual quiz (simultaneously ).Qualifying team further participated in Round-2 Bridge it.
Round-2 BRIDGE IT,a practical round in which participating teams were required to make a bridge out of popsicle sticks.The team which withstood maximum load won!
