Innovative Ideas

IEI has always been trying to bring up with new and innovative ideas through it's various events and projects.

History

The Institution of Engineers (India) (IEI), was established in 1920 and was sanctioned the Royal Charter in 1935, laying down the principal role to be of a learned society promoting the advancement of science and practice in different fields of engineering.

Vision

IEI's Vision is to enable practice of ethical ethos and pursuits of professional excellence for engineering fraternity to serve the humanity through inclusive growth.

Message from President
